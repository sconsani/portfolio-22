I am from the San Francisco Bay Area and graduated from UCLA with a degree in Fine Arts. My previous focus in art and design led me to a 5 year career in graphic design, product photography and website content management.
As I transitioned into managing the two retail websites I discovered my interests in problem solving, user experience and translating my knowledge of design into beautifully coded websites.

When I'm not creating, I love to travel, go camping, skateboarding, juggling, and reading books about psychology and neuroscience.

I value a company that is inspired and driven by personal growth, as well as excited to improve on and build a product that ultimately contributes to the wellness of the world. I care about the work I choose to do because I believe strongly in the ripple effect of my own positive attitude, problem solving mindset and desire for great change will inspire others into their own greatness as well.
